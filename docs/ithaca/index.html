Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, Ithaca Browser is completely free to download and use. We believe in open access to privacy-focused technology. In the future, we may offer premium features for power users, but the core functionality will always remain free.
Ithaca includes multiple layers of privacy protection: built-in tracker blocking, encrypted DNS, private browsing by default, and our AI agent that helps you understand and control what data you're sharing. All processing happens locally on your device whenever possible.
Ithaca is built on an open-source browser engine that supports most Chrome extensions. You can install your favorite extensions from the Chrome Web Store. However, we recommend being selective as some extensions may impact performance or privacy.
Our AI agent uses on-device machine learning to understand your browsing habits and preferences. It can help with tasks like summarizing articles, finding better deals, managing your tabs, and protecting your privacy—all while keeping your data on your device. The more you use it, the better it gets at anticipating your needs.
